Why RiseUP?

Everything You Need to Showcase Young Football Talent

From secure profile creation to professional scout discovery — all in one guardian-approved platform.

🛡️

Guardian-First Privacy

Every profile is fully controlled by a verified guardian. Scouts can only view what you choose to share.

🎥

Highlight Clip Uploads

Upload match footage and training clips directly. Share links to existing YouTube or Vimeo highlights too.

🔍

Verified Scout Network

All scouts are ID-verified by our admin team before gaining platform access. No anonymous browsing.

🏆

Smart Talent Matching

Scouts filter by age, position, and London borough. Your player gets seen by the right people.

💬

Text-Only Messaging

Scouts and guardians communicate through secure, admin-monitored text messages. No calls or video chats.

🔐

Admin Moderation

A dedicated admin team reviews all flagged content and keeps the platform safe for young players at all times.

1

Guardian Registers

2

Create Player Profile

Add your player's details — name, age, position, club, borough, and a short bio.

3

Upload Match Clips
